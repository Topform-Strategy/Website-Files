Health Assessment

It starts with a simple model …

Step 1: Data Gathering

Compilation of full application inventory. Applications are then plotted on a value chain map.

Step 2: Assessment

Multi-layered assessment of all tiers of the application. Assessment is based on an objective scale built leveraging industry best practices. Custom weights are assigned to the scores based on your internal context

Step 3: Evaluation

Generation of the health scorecard. Applications are plotted on a Business value map

Typically, our Health Assessment engagements span between eight and eleven weeks

Strategic Plan

It starts with a simple model …

Strategic planning is all about setting a target and charting a course towards the target.

Four simple steps that drive the Strategic Plan:

  • Objective setting - Through a series of Executive interviews
  • Analysis - External, Internal and SWOT
  • Choice - Generic and low level Strategy variants
  • Strategic Plan - a report that ties all of the data gathered, completed assessments, results gleaned and the strategic choices to make, to achieve the objective.

Our Strategic plan engagements usually are nine and fourteen weeks in duration.



Cloud Strategy

It starts with a simple model …

The why: The assessment phase involves understanding the drivers for cloud adoption in the context of your organization – the value cloud adoption brings to your organization. Along with the appetite for adoption, this forms the foundation for the next phase.

The what: A Cloud operating model lays out the essential elements required within your organization, required to successfully execute on the Cloud Strategy.

The how: A framework for evaluating application suitability for Cloud allows you to select a path for “new” applications and migrating existing applications to the cloud.

Our Cloud Strategy engagements usually are nine and fourteen weeks in duration.
